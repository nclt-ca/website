## Transforming the old Jam Factory Building into a space for Nelson's Creatives

Nelson, BC is a haven for artistsâ€”woodworkers, potters, painters, and makers of all kinds. Yet our creative community lacks dedicated public workspace.

The Jam Factory Building offers 25,000 square feet of heritage character, ready to become a vibrant, community-owned studio and co-working space where creators and artists can work, collaborate, and thrive together.

NCLT is a non-profit community land trust. Membership is open to anyone living in the local area. The organization is accountable to its membership, putting the building into community ownership.
