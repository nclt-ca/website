---
title: "606 Arts Space"
---

606 Arts Space is the working name for the former Kootenay School of the Arts building in downtown Nelson, located at 606 Victoria Street.

![606 address sign](/images/606-arts-space.jpeg)

Beginning August 1, 2026, Nelson Community Land Trust will hold a five-year lease for the building and support its transition into a shared arts, culture, education, and community space.

The building will operate with multiple tenants. [Kootenay School of the Arts Society (KSAS)](https://ksarts.ca/) will be one of them, continuing KSA educational programming in the building alongside other arts and community organizations.
