Kallee Lins is the Executive Director of the West Kootenay Regional Arts Council, bringing specialized expertise in network-driven cultural development and arts advocacy across the Columbia Basin. With a PhD from York University and extensive experience in non-profit leadership, Lins has dedicated her career to advancing equitable access to arts and culture. She is Board Chair of Dance West Network and an inaugural board member of the Black & Rural Arts and Culture Society.
