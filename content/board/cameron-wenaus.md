Cameron is the co-founder and former CEO of [Retreat Guru](https://retreat.guru), which he and his brother Deryk grew into a successful international business over more than a decade. He holds an MBA from Dalhousie University and a BA in Psychology from UBC.

He is currently CEO of the [Synthesis Institute](https://www.synthesisinstitute.com), a professional training organisation. Cameron also established the Kootenay Shambhala Meditation Centre in Nelson and was a founding member of the original Jam Factory coworking space.
