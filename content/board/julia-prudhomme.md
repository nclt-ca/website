Julia Prudhomme is the Executive Director of Oxygen Art Centre in Nelson, a role she has held since 2019. She holds an MFA in Interdisciplinary Studies from UBC and is a doctoral candidate at McMaster University, with a curatorial practice focused on artistic process and experimentation. Julia brings over a decade of experience working in public art galleries and artist-run centres.
