Matt is a software entrepreneur based in Nelson. He co-founded Cucumber Limited, growing it to $1.5M in annual recurring revenue before a successful acquisition by SmartBear, a US software company, in 2019.

He is currently a Staff Software Engineer and Engineering Manager at [Mechanical Orchard](https://www.mechanicalorchard.com), and brings experience in building and scaling a profitable international business from Nelson.
