Matt is a software engineer, author, and public speaker based in Nelson. He co-founded Cucumber Limited, a business built around the widely-used open source [Cucumber](https://cucumber.io) testing tool, which was successfully acquired by SmartBear, a US software company, in 2019.

A long-time contributor to open source software, he is currently a Staff Software Engineer and Engineering Manager at [Mechanical Orchard](https://www.mechanicalorchard.com).
