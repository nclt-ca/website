Matt is a software engineer based in Nelson. He co-founded a business to support the widely-used open source [Cucumber](https://cucumber.io) testing tool, which was acquired in 2019. He is currently a Staff Software Engineer and Engineering Manager at [Mechanical Orchard](https://www.mechanicalorchard.com). He'd rather be up a mountain though. He served as technical director of the Kootenay Mountaineering Club and is a founding member of the NCLT.
